This web page is created for design purposes only. The concept and the producer displayed on this web page is entirely fanmade and does not represent an actual product or a company

EXPLORE THE ANCIENT LAND OF CEYLON

Wander through the abyss of tropical rainforests or stroll down the ancient lake cities built around sky reaching pagodas. Conquer the hill country and step into the realm of the god of Mount Samanala. Lionborne includes an open-world expansive terrain which replicates the scenaries and locations of ancient Sri Lanka

SHED LION BLOOD, AVENGE THE MOTHERLAND

In Lionborne you play as a Sri Lankan mercenary warrior who carries the duty of protecting the heir to the throne, young prince Kashyapa. Being skilled in the local martial art of Angampora you must take on the Indian invaders to avenge the fallen kingdom and restore the throne. In the late king's words; Motherland comes first

SLAY BEASTS AND DOMINATE THE WILDS

The game sets in a period of time where the lands of Ceylon was ruled by four godly creatures of power. Thuranga the Red eyed Stallion, Hanuma the Warrior Ape, Naiyadi the Great Cobra and Gajaga the King of Elephants. With the blessing of the four eternals Vishnu, Saman, Katharagama and Pattini you may bring these monsters to rest and free the people
